Introducing the RobotBulls Coin

By owning Bitcoin you dont get any other benefits from the coin than just owning it. If the price rises you can exchange it back to more fiat currency. And this is assuming that you want to change it back to fiat.

RobotBulls allows you to get a percentage from the revenue of the decentralized platform. There are actually two ways of benefiting from the coin:

  • The first one is just keeping the coin in your wallet. And if you keep it in your wallet the protocols and the smart contracts involved are going to send to your wallet more coins once in a year. You can think of it as the equivalent of a dividend.
  • The other way to benefit from the coin is to place it in one of the Bulls. We will come to speak on this point later.

Key Features

Yearly Dividends

Coin holders get a once in a year dividend for holding the coin.

Powering Ai Trading Bots

The Coin helps the platform to train trading robots.

Leveraging Miners Computing Power

We use miners' computing power to train our models and run our software.
