Taipei Talk about AI

RobotBulls is embarking on its inaugural journey to Taiwan, aiming to assess the potential for expanding into the Taiwanese market.

  • 30 January 2024
  • Metodi Dimitrov, Robin Krambröckers, Cheng-Lin Hsu-Dayen, Kristina Sutkaityte

Insights from January 30th

RobotBulls recently marked its first foray into the Taiwanese market with the successful "Taipei Talk about AI" event, held at Trattoria Tre in Taipei's vibrant Neihu District. This groundbreaking gathering aimed to explore the interest and opportunities in leveraging artificial intelligence for trading bots in Taiwan.

The "Taipei Talk about AI" event has set a precedent for future discussions and developments in the Taiwanese trading sector, leaving a lasting impression on all those who participated.
